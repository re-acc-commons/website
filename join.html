What We're Building

The commons is actively working on infrastructure for regenerative acceleration:

Agent Infrastructure

AI agents that participate in governance, manage treasury, and coordinate work. We're pioneering human-AI collaboration at the organizational level.

Knowledge Commons

Shared resources for regenerative projects. Documentation, patterns, and learnings that accelerate everyone's work.

Treasury Infrastructure

Multi-sig treasury with AI participation. Experimenting with regenerative funding mechanisms and resource allocation.

Coordination Tools

Governance systems, consent mechanisms, and collaboration infrastructure. The operating system for the commons.

For Everyone

You don't need to code to contribute. The commons needs:

  • Writers to document processes and share knowledge
  • Organizers to coordinate events and working groups
  • Researchers to explore ideas and synthesize learnings
  • Facilitators to guide discussions and decision-making
  • Connectors to build bridges with other communities
  • Artists to visualize and communicate the vision

Every contribution matters. Start small, show up consistently, and the path forward will emerge.
